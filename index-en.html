About us

Our Company "A. Psathas and Sons" was founded in 1960 with headquarters in the Komotini, Greece. Its object is the manufacture of all kinds of vehicle covers from laminated (coated) fabrics, as well as tents and awnings.

Our company having significant experience in the manufacture of tents and vehicle covers from laminated fabrics, and has invested huge amounts not only in the acquisition of expertise and mechanical equipment but also in the acquisition of certifications since they are a prerequisite to take part in tenders or to sign contracts. Furthermore the company has building premises that meet the production's requirements.

We have traveled extensively and worked with leading companies, equipment makers and fabric suppliers in our company. This has given us valuable insights into the best practices, materials and tools for making tents and awnings. We partner with reputable European factories to source high-quality raw materials and develop innovative products.

After the investments we made, our company is located in a 1,500 m² private property, which includes offices, production areas, and storage facilities. We have state-of-the-art mechanical equipment and and highly skilled technicians.

Manufacture of tank-vehicle-weapon covers

Our Company is based in Komotini, a strategic location for the national defense. We have been innovating with synthetic, PVC-coated fabric (or “plastic fabric”) for the Armed Forces. In 1994, we were the first company to make covers for armored vehicles for the 4th Army Corps units.

Due to its successful participation in public tenders, the Company has undertaken the construction of covers for armored vehicles and vehicles in numerous military units throughout the country.

We use materials that meet the Hellenic Army’s standards (ΠΓΕΣ-ΚΚΥ-1305/07-2001), as confirmed by the Army’s chemical tests, ensuring they meet operational requirements. Moreover, the company represents and processes various types of PVC-coated fabrics from European manufacturers that comply with NATO's terms and specifications. European collaborations include the company "Sattler" from Austria.

Over the past 25 years, we have been the leading supplier of covers to the Hellenic Army, winning almost 80% of the contracts through legitimate procedures (tenders). We owe this success to our superior materials and craftsmanship, as well as our affordable prices.

We also make and deliver covers for civil protection shelters, with customized solutions for different weather challenges, such as storms, heat waves, and other emergencies. We guarantee that our shelters are safe and dependable.
